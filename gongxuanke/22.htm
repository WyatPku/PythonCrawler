先修课程 概率论，高等数学
中文简介 互联网与大数据技术的兴起，让服务业在国民经济中所占GDP的比重越来越高。服务系统面临着从机制设计，运作模式优化，到运营层面效率有效益提升的多种目标。为消费者提供多样高质的服务成为服务行业越来越关注的问题。服务系统的优化运作与决策管理涉及到包括复杂供给网络的设计，预算约束，组织结构的流程优化等多目标的协同优化问题。
本课程将给学生介绍服务系统的优化运作建模与决策管理研究的经典理论和最新的前沿学术研究成果。通过对以电商、医疗、零售业、和能源服务等服务系统的讲解来启发学生该如何针对服务系统进行优化设计与改进。课程中将涉及到的理论工具包括运筹学优化建模、经济成本效用分析、博弈及序贯优化决策等。通过这些方法来研究如何在有限的资源约束条件下最优化的对服务系统进行资源配置与优化运作研究。通过课程的学习，学生将了解并掌握基本的优化决策建模分析方法，以此达到优化服务系统的服务供给，增大企业的效益，提高消费者的满意度。
英文简介 Service organizations have been undergone profound reforms in the ear pf Internet, aiming to increase efficiency, decrease waste, and reshape the way that service was delivered. Providing high-quality for improved organization and management in service systems. These systems include public and private service corporations that are complex networks of human, financial, structural and technological resources with their objective to guarantee public service demand.
In this course, students will be introduced to operations management models of service systems. This course explores opportunities for improvement in the design and management of healthcare, retail and supply chain service system. Since The course utilizes tools such as optimization modelling, economic analysis, decision analysis. Since we face resource allocation choices all the time. Think of the trade-offs among worthy social spending. Cost-effectiveness analysis is the useful tool that helps to examine how to make the decisions about the best use of limited resources in service system. The course aim to let the students understand the uses and basic components of CEA and the basic steps in conducting a CEA in service systems’ decisions, also the use of process models for improving productivity and profitability,also the satisfaction from consumers.
