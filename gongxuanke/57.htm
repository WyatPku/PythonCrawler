课程号 00333116 学分 3
英文名称 CubeSat control and simulation
先修课程 控制原理；理论力学；
中文简介 帮助学生运用已经学过的控制和动力学知识，进一步掌握飞行器建模、辨识和控制器设计技术，理解经典控制理论和飞行器设计问题，并通过实际上机实验和课程设计锻炼实际分析和解决工程问题能力。

本暑期会关注一个主题：微小卫星控制仿真。
英文简介 The objective of this course is to introduce the fundamentals of flight vehicle modeling; identification and controller design techniques, and help students to understand the classical control and its applications in flight controller. The students are expected to grasp hand-on experiences with practical project works. The contents of the course are covering multi-disciplines, which include flight modeling, stability analysis, control theory, flight simulation and code developments.

One of the topics to be studied in this term will be about CubeSat (nano satellite control and simulation).
