中文简介 利用在北京和中国其他城市的出色博物馆里丰富的艺术资源，本课程将通过直接研究这些博物馆的杰作，介绍从古代到当代的中国艺术。通过对艺术作品的深入研究，学生对当今中国文化和社会将有深入的了解。本课程的主题包括古老的仪式，精英文化，佛教图标，前卫艺术，流行艺术和建筑环境。 我们将在星期一，星期二和星期五在课堂上举行讲座和展开讨论。星期四，我们将在北京的博物馆和艺术画廊举行会议，参观艺术品，并与策展人见面。我们将在7月23 至27日选择去苏州，乌镇，杭州或上海中的一个或多个城市参观博物馆和历史遗迹。
英文简介 Taking advantage of the rich resources of Chinese art in the fantastic museums in Beijing and other cities in China, this course introduces Chinese art from ancient to contemporary times by directly examining masterpieces in these museums. Through a thorough study of the artworks, students develop an in-depth understanding about Chinese culture and society today. The topics of this course include ancient rituals, elite culture, Buddhist icons, avant-garde, popular art, and built environment. We will meet in classroom for lectures and discussions on Mondays, Tuesdays, and Fridays. On Thursdays, we will meet in museums and art galleries around Beijing to examine artworks and meet with curators. An optional trip to Suzhou, Wuzhen, Hangzhou and Shanghai to visit museums and historical sites will take place from Jul 23-27.
