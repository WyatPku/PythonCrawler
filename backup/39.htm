中文简介 该课程旨在为具有工程或科学背景的本科生讲授燃烧科学和工程的相关知识。我们将讨论燃烧科学的各个方面，涵盖燃烧工程中的许多实际问题。 在本课程中，学生将获得关于燃烧及其应用的基本知识，例如实际的燃料，燃料燃烧的热化学和化学动力学，非预混和预混火焰（燃烧）的特性和稳定性，火焰的点燃和熄灭，各种燃烧中的能量转换装置，以及燃烧的环境影响。通过这门课程，学生们将对燃料，燃烧过程，燃烧装置和污染物排放之间的关系有一个充分的了解。
英文简介 The course aims to introduce combustion science and engineering to undergraduate students with background in engineering or science. Various aspects of combustion science will be discussed and many practical issues in combustion engineering will be covered. In the course, the students will obtain basic knowledge on combustion and its applications, such as practical fuels, thermochemistry and chemical kinetics of fuel combustion, characteristics and stability of non-premixed and premixed flames (combustion), ignition and flame extinction, various combustion energy conversion devices, and environmental effects of combustion. By taking this course, the students are expected to develop a sound understanding of relationships among fuels, combustion processes, combustion devices and pollutant emissions.

stabilization.
？ Gas-fired furnaces and boilers; Premixed-charge engine combustion.

？ Coal-fired furnace combustion; Chimney and flue.
