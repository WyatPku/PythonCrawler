中文简介 本课程的目的是让学生理解神经假体的原理和前沿。神经假体是一种植入的与神经系统交互的电子系统。通过直接以电信号刺激神经系统,它可以帮助神经系统恢复受损或缺失的感觉和运动功能。典型的例子包括最近开发的分别应用于严重听力和视力受损病人的耳蜗植入和视网膜植入。最近，与大脑中的神经元的交互吸引了临床治疗和科学研究两方面的关注。这门课将涵盖听觉假体、视觉假体和脑深部电刺激中的工程问题。
英文简介 The aim of this course is to understand the principles and state-of-the arts development of the Neural Prosthesis. Neural prosthesis is an electronic implant that interfaces with nervous systems. Through direct electrical stimulation of nerves, it can help restore damaged or lost sensory or motion functions. Typical examples include cochlear implant and retina implant recently developed for severely hearing and vision impaired patients respectively. More recently interfacing with neurons in brain draws more attention for both therapeutic and scientific purposes. In this lecture we will cover all engineering aspects of the auditory, visual prostheses, and deep brain stimulation.
