中文简介 人类生存与发展的刚性需求使得对资源和环境的压力持续加大，全球性环境问题日益突出。人类以往所依靠的高消耗、高污染的传统发展道路显然已走不通。可持续发展是世界经济发展的必由之路。现今，世界各国正掀起以绿色技术为核心的产业革命，努力探索最佳能源利用、工业重塑的方法，寻找创建绿色高效的未来智慧社会的途径。

本课程力图将环境科学、环境工程的基础知识与科技、人文、社会科学的理念结合起来，引领学生了解世界能源格局、中国能源问题，激发学生对未来社会的构想和思辨能力，使学生在交叉综合的教学氛围和实践行动中，和教员一起探讨各种未来社会发展解决方案的可行性。希望本课程能开拓学生的全球化视野，激励学生对世界能源、经济、科技发展的关心和主人翁精神，培养学生绿色的、着眼未来的可持续发展观。

本课程将设置以下四个专题：（1）摆脱化石能源依赖；（2）更高明的交通系统；（3）绿色建筑和低碳生活；（4）地球工程学。课程包括课堂讲授和实践教学两个部分。课堂讲授的重点是能源的基础知识、全球能源及中国能源概况、能源带来的环境问题，介绍绿色技术和绿色理念。实践教学包括两次野外实习：（1）组织学生参观北大理科楼顶的空气质量长期监测站点，了解我国在大气污染防治方面所做的努力；（2）组织学生前往京郊的风力发电、太阳能发电、蓄能电厂参观，了解可持续的能源解决方案。
英文简介 The rigid demands of the human's activity and the economic development put tremendous pressure on natural resources and the environment. It has lead to more and more serious global environmental issues. The traditional developing pathway that relies on high consumption and leads to high pollution would not work for our current world any more. Sustainable development is the only way for the future of the world economy. Today, many countries are eagerly searching for green technologies for the industrial revolution and are striving to try various ways to perform energy and industrial remodeling. All aims at creating a green and efficient future wisdom of urban city design.

This course attempts to combine the concepts of environmental science and environmental engineering with the basic knowledge of science and technology, humanities, and social sciences, to lead students to understand the potential solutions towards sustainable development from the new angle of view. The students will be guided to explore various ideas of future social development under an interdisciplinary, comprehensive atmosphere and to outline the future life of beautiful blueprint. This course aims at stimulating the passion and thinking of the students about our future planet, to open up the students' global perspective, and to inspire students to care for the world economy and the development of science and technology.

This course contains four topics: (1) deduction of fossil energy dependence; (2) more sophisticated transport system; (3) green building and low carbon life; (4) geo-engineering. It includes two parts, classroom teaching and practical training. Classroom teaching focuses on introducing more efficient, cleaner, more economical production and consumption patterns, as well as inspired ideas about future development. Practice training include two field trips. Two field trips will be arranged as part of the class hours. One is to visit the PKU air-quality monitoring roof site (2 class hours). The other is to visit the wind farm, solar power, and pumped hydro energy storage facilities in the suburb of Beijing (6 class hours).
